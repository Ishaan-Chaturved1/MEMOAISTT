The Lord of the Rings is an epic fantasy story about Frodo Baggins, a simple hobbit who is entrusted with destroying a powerful ring created by the dark lord Sauron. Accompanied by loyal friends and allies, he journeys across the dangerous lands of Middle-earth while a great war unfolds between good and evil. As the ring’s corrupting power grows stronger, Frodo struggles to complete his mission, but through courage, sacrifice, and friendship, the ring is ultimately destroyed, leading to Sauron’s defeat and the restoration of peace.
